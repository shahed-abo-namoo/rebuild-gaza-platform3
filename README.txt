# Rebuild Gaza Platform

A front-end web platform designed to guide families in Gaza through the process of home reconstruction after damage, providing clear stages, cost estimation, and access to volunteer engineers.

---

##  Project Overview

**Rebuild Gaza** is an informational and guidance platform that aims to:
- Explain the stages of home reconstruction in a simple and clear way.
- Provide an approximate cost calculator based on area, damage level, floors, and material quality.
- Connect affected families with volunteer engineers for free consultations.
- Offer a user-friendly Arabic RTL interface accessible on all devices.
